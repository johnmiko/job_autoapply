Bachelor's degree in Engineering (Electrical or Computer Science)

Ability to work productively with colleagues and cross functional teams
Proficient in leading technical activities

Excellent verbal and written communication skills as it pertains to articulating, documenting and presenting findings and recommendations
Nice-to-Have:

Training certification in ISO 26262
Functional safety ASIC development experience
